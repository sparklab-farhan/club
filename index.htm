The Women’s Bridge

Here’s to Our Clubs History.. The La Puente Valley Woman’s Club started informally as a book club of about one dozen women who had meetings at La Puente High School, said rene hrey president of the club. The original member’ husband and others built them a space of their own in 1923 and they started organizing community events. the main purpose is to help the community. the club will host fundraisers to help verterams, the homeless, familes in need, as well as provide scholarship for high school students that attend local colleges. The General Federation of women’s Club, founded in 1890, is the umbrella group for smaller women’s around the country. there are currently over 3,000 active clubs with 100,000 active members throughout the country. Today, the clubs continue working on their mission of community improvement through volunteer service.
